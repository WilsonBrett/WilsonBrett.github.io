Chilis

Everybody's Meat Chili

Everybody's Meat Chili is a traditional home made chili recipe with red and black beans, grass fed organic beef, a tomato sauce base, various spices, and shredded cheese.

CUP: $4.95

BOWL: $6.95

Everybody's Veggie Chili

Everybody's Veggie Chili is the same as the meat chili without the beef, but with added bell peppers and mushrooms!

CUP: $3.95

BOWL: $5.95

Santa Fe Chili

Our Santa Fe Chili is straight out of New Mexico with beef and black beans seasoned to perfection and topped with green chilis and shredded cheese.

CUP: $5.50

BOWL: $7.25

Where to Find Us

In Season: Memorial Day - Labor Day

We operate weekdays in Portland's Monument Square from 11am-3pm and on Commercial St. from 4pm-8pm. Please also check our Facebook page for local events and festivals.

Off Season: Labor Day - Memorial Day

On weekdays in the off season after labor day we will be primarily located in Portland's Monument Square from 11am-3pm.
